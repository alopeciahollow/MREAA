Our Culture

Members Helping Members

The 30-Day Rule

Members commit to responding to networking or professional inquiries from fellow alumni and current students within 30 days.

The Hiring Preference

Members are encouraged to share job openings within the Association's network before listing them publicly — strengthening our talent pipeline.

The Chatham House Standard

Insights shared at our events may travel; the identity of who shared them may not. Designated Forums carry absolute confidentiality.

Application

Membership Application

Voting membership is restricted to MRE and MDes (REBE) graduates. Current students and allied applicants with a demonstrated real estate focus may apply for associate or affiliate tiers.

Submissions are sent to membership@mreaa.org. We review applications within five business days.
