Recurring Programs

Throughout the Year

Peer-Advisory Forums

Designated closed roundtables where confidentiality is absolute and members speak strictly from personal experience — a trusted space for real decisions.

Mentorship Cohorts

Structured, year-long pairings of established practitioners with current students. Applications for mentors and mentees open each summer.

Student & Community Events

Open industry panels, site visits, and student mixers — offered at least twice annually and open to students of any institution and the general public.
